Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how your data is handled in connection with Gmail and Google services used by Voice Based Email Sender.

1. What Google Collects

Google collects data such as device info, location, app usage, search and watch history, and email content (used for features like spam detection and Smart Compose).

2. How Data Is Used

  • To improve features like Gmail spam filters and suggestions
  • To personalize user experiences, recommendations, and ads
  • To enhance security and detect abuse (e.g., phishing, spam)

3. Data Protection

Google uses encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Workspace accounts may have optional end-to-end encryption for Gmail content.

4. User Controls

You have control over your data via:

  • Privacy Checkup to review Google’s data collection
  • Settings to disable Smart Features in Gmail
  • Ability to revoke third-party app permissions

5. Data Sharing

Google does not sell your personal data. Limited data may be shared with service providers or for legal compliance.

6. Retention and Deletion

You can manage how long data is stored. Inactive Gmail accounts (2+ years) may be suspended or deleted, but auto-delete controls are available.
